About Me

NISHANT

My name is Nishant, and I am a final year student at NIT Kurukshetra, pursuing a B.Tech degree in Electrical Engineering. Throughout my academic journey, I have actively engaged in various extracurricular activities that have honed my skills in discipline, confidence, leadership, and management.

As an Under Officer in NCC and Joint Secretary in the Electroreck Society, I have had the opportunity to demonstrate my leadership abilities while contributing to the growth and success of these organizations. Additionally, being a member of the Student Council at NIT Kurukshetra has further developed my skills in managing and representing the student body.

Apart from my academic and leadership pursuits, I am an avid sports enthusiast, and my favorite sport is field hockey. Playing sports has taught me valuable lessons in teamwork, perseverance, and dedication. I am passionate about the field of electrical engineering and eager to contribute my knowledge and skills to make a meaningful impact in the industry.
